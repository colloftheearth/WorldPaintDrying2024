About the Competition

The World Championship of Paint Drying is an international event where contestants face off in a battle of concentration and patience. Each competitor is assigned a 1x1 meter wall covered in "WCPD Certified Paint." The goal is simple: **observe, document, and endure.**

Competition Rules

  • Contestants must remain focused on their wall at all times.
  • No phones, books, or distractions allowed.
  • Observation notes must include "scientific terminology" like "Flash-Off Point" and "Micro-Crackle Formation."
  • Bonus points for successfully completing the "No-Blink Challenge" (5 minutes without blinking).
